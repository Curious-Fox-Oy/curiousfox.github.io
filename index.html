About

Small team. Big experience.

Curious Fox is a software development studio founded by two senior engineers, each with over 20 years in the industry. We've shipped products for startups, scaled systems for enterprises, and led teams through complex technical challenges.

Our strength lies in breadth without sacrificing depth. We're comfortable architecting a system from scratch, diving into legacy code, or stepping into a leadership role when projects need direction. We've been developers, tech leads, and trusted advisors — often all at once.

We take on a limited number of engagements at a time. This means we can give your project the focus it deserves, and you get direct access to senior expertise — no layers, no hand-offs.

We embrace AI as a core part of how we work. While our decades of experience guide the architecture and decisions, AI tools accelerate the implementation — letting us deliver faster without compromising on quality.
